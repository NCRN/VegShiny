Species Lists

These lists show the scientfic (Latin) and common names of plant species known from the parks.

Plants in the Monitoring Plots

This list displays all of the vascular plants species found during forest monitoring. By default, this is shown for all of the plots in a park. If you want to see a list for only certain plots, you can delete "All Plots" from the "Plots (optional)" control and choose one or more plots. The name of a plot can found by can be found by hovering over it on the 'Map' panel.


All Vascular Plants Known from the Park

The information presented in these species lists are subsets of data managed in NPSpecies, which is the master biodiversity database for the National Park Service. The species lists presented here have all gone through a certification process. This involves the evaluation and review of the species information by local experts who are well-versed in the biology, taxonomy, and nomenclature of species in the parks. For more information, including species lists of other groups, such as birds and mammals, visit the National Captial Region Network Species List page.
